Marketing researcher (paid internship)

Google Ads, Hubspot, MailChimp, WordPress, LinkedIn, Twitter, SalesForce, Pinterest, GitHub, Google Analytics, NewRelic, KissMetrics, Google Play, Facebook, Android …

Do these sound like the shiny toys you can’t wait to play with in a real business application? It’s your chance to give them all a go, learn on the job and make a difference!

Your work will be divided into 3 parts:

  1. digging out data
  2. running an experiment
  3. measuring the results

We provide a set of problems, tools and budget. You are expected to deliver solutions.

Minimal requirements

  • attention to detail
  • immaculate presentation of your deliverables
  • good time management
  • result orientated
  • hard worker
  • relevant education, background or practical skills

Ability to create basic content is a great bonus. E.g., write an ad, a short blog post or landing page or edit an email template.

Basic CSS / HTML skill would be really nice to have too, but we can give you some basic training with that.

Working conditions

  • flexible hours
  • flexible location: Birmingham or North Shore, Auckland or from your own place wherever you are
  • minimum 20hrs a week
  • fixed term contract with an option to go permanent

Application pack

We’d like to see your CV and answers to the these questions:

  1. Why are you interested in this position?
  2. Your relevant background
  3. What professional blogs / publications / podcasts / youtube channels you follow?
  4. Name 3 technologies from the list at the top you are familiar with.
  5. Name 3 technologies you think we should be using.

Hey, this is your chance to join a young company and get to try things a corporate player would never let you to. Drop us a line on jobs @ our domain, you know.
